# CryptView
A real-time cryptocurrency portfolio tracker that helps you monitor your crypto investments, track performance, and analyze market trends across multiple exchanges and wallets.

<b>Feautures</b>
Real-time Portfolio Tracking - Monitor your crypto holdings with live price updates
Profit/Loss Analysis - Track gains and losses across all your investments
Price Charts & Analytics - Visualize historical performance and market trends
Multi-Exchange Support - Connect multiple wallets and exchange accounts
Multi-Currency Support - View your portfolio value in different fiat currencies
Price Alerts - Get notified when your assets hit target prices
Responsive Design - Access your portfolio on desktop, tablet, or mobile
